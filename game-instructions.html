What is Pokergraphic Memory?

Pokergraphic Memory is a challenging memory training game that tasks you to first memorise a randomly ordered deck of playing cards, before requiring you to correctly recall the sequence. Your performance is assessed through two criteria; your time to memorise the deck, and your number of correctly recalled cards.

How to play!

  1. Click the Start Game chip and choose a difficulty. Easy is 10 cards, medium is 25, and hard is all 52 cards!
  2. Click the Start Timer button to begin the clock and present the first card.
  3. Once you have memorised the first card simply click the Next Card button to present a new card.
  4. After all the cards have been presented to you click the Finish Memorising! button to stop the timer.
  5. The game will now allow you to input your card choices. Close the window by clicking the Start Guessing! button.
  6. To input your choices just click the cards in the correct order.
  7. Click the 'Mute Audio" button at any time to mute or un-mute the sound effects.
  8. Click the 'Previous Score' button to check your previous results.
