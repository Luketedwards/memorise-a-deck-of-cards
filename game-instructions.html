What is Memorise a Deck of Cards?

Memorise a Deck of Cards is a challenging memory training game that tasks you to first memorise a randomly ordered deck of playing cards, before requiring you to correctly recall the sequence. Your performance is assessed through two criteria; your time to memorise the deck, and your number of correctly recalled cards.

How to play!

  1. Click the Start Game chip to shuffle the cards and open the memorisation window.
  2. Click the Start Timer button to begin the clock and present the first card.
  3. Once you have memorised the first card simply click the Next Card button to present a new card.
  4. After all 52 cards have been presented to you click the Finish Memorising! button to stop the timer.
  5. The game will now allow you to input your card choices. Close the window by clicking the Start Guessing! button.
  6. To input your choices just click the cards in the correct order.
